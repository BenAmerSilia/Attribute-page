ABOUT

Born on January 24, 1956 in At Dwala, Haute Kabylie, is a Kabyle singer and militant of the Berber cause. At the age of twenty-two, he recorded his first album called "A yizem" which was a huge success. Lounès expresses himself in his songs openly against the fascism of the Algerian power and the Islamist movement, which earned him several assassination attempts.

    Assassination

    On 25 June 1998, at approximately 12:30 pm local time, Matoub's car was stopped and was fired upon by masked gunmen, killing Matoub. Thousands of angry mourners gathered around the hospital where his body was taken and shouted "Pouvoir, Assassin"
